South Indian Hill Stations

  • Kaivalya Naik
  • Web Design
  • 14

When summers hit South India with scorching heat and humidity, the enchanting hill stations with incredible landscapes and pleasantly cold climate comes to the rescue making South India a fabulous summer getaway to beat the unbearable heat. Holidaying become a fascinating and rejuvenating experience during summers especially in such pristine and picturesque hill stations in South India which are sprinkled with lush greenery, scenic locales, sprawling tea and spice plantations, emerald lakes, ravishing waterfalls, gorgeous valleys and exotic flora and fauna. Numerous hill stations are there in South India that offers a delightful setting for a wonderful and memorable vacation all year-round. Here we have listed some of the Best Hill Stations To Visit In South India During Summer. Check out!

Munnar, one of the most popular and picturesque hill stations in Kerala, tops the list of best hill stations to visit in South India during summer. It is beautifully set amidst the Kannan Devan Hills and overlooks lush tea plantations. The scenic landscapes, never-ending carpets of greenery, craggy peaks, delightful ambiance, refreshing air, abundant wildlife and all-embracing natural beauty make Munnar an ultimate cradle of nature that offers utter bliss and respite.

Ooty is one of the mesmerizing travel destinations in South India to spend vacations away from the scorching summer sun. Nestled deep within the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, the lovely hill station of Ooty enjoys a cooler climate during the summer. The rustic old-world charm, lush greenery, lofty waterfalls, spiraling water streams, spellbinding natural sceneries, and amazing tranquillity attracts flocks of tourists to Ooty. Especially a thrilling ride on the Nilgiri Heritage Mountain Train will be a richly rewarding experience on your trip to Ooty.

Kudremukh is a picture-perfect hill station with cool delightful weather that offers an absolute heavenly setting to relax and unwind during the summer. It serves as a part of the mighty Western Ghats and is situated in the Chikmagalur district of Karnataka. Famed for its rich biodiversity and enchanting natural beauty, Kudremukh is full of emerald dense forest, mountainous paths with scenic views and rolling grasslands embellished with rare varieties of orchids and gorgeous rivers.

This summer, beat the heat, relax and rejuvenate and enjoy a fun-filled vacation at the beautiful hill stations of South India.
